Birthday Cake

A colorful, sweet addition to your family's birthday party.

Filled with rainbow sprinkles and delicious vanilla flavoring, this cake is topped with a homemade vanilla icing.

Ingredients

  • Funfetti Sprinkles
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 Degrees
  2. Mix together 4 cups flour with 2 eggs and a stick of butter.
  3. Add 1 cup sugar to flour mixture
  4. Add milk and vanilla Extract to the flour mixture
  5. Once fully combined, evenly disperse the sprinkles throughout the batter
  6. Bake in over for 30-35 minutes
