Chocolate Chip Cookies

A sweet, scrumptious cookie never tasted before

As a kid, my mom used to make chocolate chip cookies. These are a vast improvement on those with the addition of dark chocolate chunks and sea salt. Yum!

Ingredients

  • Dark Chocolate Bar
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Brown Sugar
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 Degrees
  2. Mix together 4 cups flour with 2 eggs and a stick of butter.
  3. Add 1 cup brown sugar and 1 cup sugar to flour mixture
  4. Add milk to the flour mixture
  5. Once fully combined, break the chocolate bar into pieces and fold into cookies
  6. Bake in over for 10-12 minutes
