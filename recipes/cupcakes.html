Birthday Cake

Vanilla Cupcakes with decadent strawberry icing are sure to impress!

These vanilla cupcakes are topped with our signature homemade strawberry icing. Made from fresh strawberries and cream.

Ingredients

  • Strawberries
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Sugar
  • Heavy Cream

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 Degrees
  2. Mix together 4 cups flour with 2 eggs and a stick of butter.
  3. Add 1 cup sugar to flour mixture
  4. Add milk and vanilla Extract to the flour mixture
  5. Once fully combined, evenly disperse some of the strawberries in the batter
  6. Bake in over for 30-35 minutes
  7. While Baking, combine the strawberries with 1/2 cup heavy cream anbd 4 cups of sugar. Blend Until smooth and creamy.
